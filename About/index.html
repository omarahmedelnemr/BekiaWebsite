Vision:

الرؤية:

To lead the transformation towards an innovative waste management solutions that reduce environmental impact, conserve natural resources, and create economic opportunities for communities.

قيادة التحول نحو حلول مبتكرة لإدارة المخلفات تعمل على تقليل الأثر البيئي، والحفاظ على الموارد الطبيعية، وخلق فرص اقتصادية للمجتمعات.

Mission:

الرسالة:

Our mission is to establish a comprehensive waste management system that empowers communities to actively participate in recycling and sustainable practices. By leveraging advanced technology and efficient processes, we aim to reduce carbon emissions, conserve resources, and promote a circular economy, contributing to a cleaner and healthier environment for future generations.

مهمتنا هي إنشاء نظام شامل لإدارة المخلفات يعمل على تمكين المجتمعات من المشاركة بنشاط في إعادة التدوير والممارسات المستدامة. من خلال الاستفادة من التكنولوجيا المتقدمة والعمليات الفعّالة، نهدف إلى تقليل انبعاثات الكربون والحفاظ على الموارد وتعزيز الاقتصاد الدائري، مما يساهم في بيئة أنظف وأكثر صحة للأجيال القادمة.

Our History:

تاريخنا:

Since our launch in Mars 2024, Bekyapay has successfully reached over 1,000 homes in Alexandria and partnered with more than 20 institutions in less than one year. We have recycled approximately 115 tons of waste during this period and raised awareness about waste separation and recycling among more than 300 people. Additionally, we have repaired and resold over 100 electronic devices and pieces of furniture at highly affordable prices to support the most disadvantaged communities. Our efforts have not only provided essential items at lower costs but also significantly contributed to reducing emissions and promoting a cleaner, more sustainable environment.
