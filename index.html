BekyaPay

BekyaPay is a tech-driven startup transforming waste management in Egypt through sustainable practices. We connect businesses and individuals to efficient recycling services, our mission is to promote sustainability, reduce landfill waste, and create a cleaner environment through smart technology and community engagement.

Collecting Solid Waste

Bekyapay provides an innovative solution for collecting non-hazardous solid waste from institutions and homes, offering a passive income in the form of cash or food commodities. This model encourages responsible waste management while giving households an extra income stream to help with expenses. For businesses, including restaurants, hypermarkets, and schools, Bekyapay’s services contribute to cleaner environments while promoting sustainable practices. By turning waste into a valuable resource, the company helps both individuals and institutions reduce environmental impact and improve financial stability. 

Raising Awareness About Waste Separation

Bekyapay is committed to raising awareness about the importance of waste separation, particularly among youth. Through collaborations with schools and universities, the company organizes workshops and educational events focused on teaching students how to separate recyclable, organic, and non-recyclable waste. By engaging young people, Bekyapay fosters a culture of sustainability and responsibility that extends to their homes and communities. These efforts aim to inspire the next generation to adopt environmentally friendly habits, ultimately contributing to a cleaner and more sustainable future.

Reusing Electronic Devices

Bekyapay advocates for the reuse of electronic devices to reduce e-waste and its environmental impact. Many discarded electronics can be repaired or repurposed, extending their lifespan and reducing the need for new production. By promoting reuse, the company helps minimize the disposal of hazardous materials like lead and mercury, which can harm the environment. bBekyapay encourages individuals and businesses to explore reuse options for their electronics, ensuring that working devices are either passed on or recycled responsibly, contributing to sustainable electronic waste management.

The Importance of Furniture Reuse

Bekyapay promotes the reuse of furniture to reduce waste and environmental harm. Instead of discarding usable furniture, the company encourages donation, resale, or repurposing, which helps extend its lifecycle. This reduces the need for new resources and energy for production, while also lowering deforestation and pollution linked to manufacturing. By raising awareness around furniture reuse, bBekyapay makes it easier for individuals and businesses to contribute to sustainability. Through these efforts, the company supports a circular economy and reduces the burden on landfills.
